Backup/Restore (experimental)

Save all your WindowSizer settings so that you can restore them later! You can even save multiple different groups of settings (sort of like "profiles"), each with a memorable name of your choice.

If you have Firefox Sync enabled for Add-Ons, your backups will be saved to the Mozilla servers as well. They will then be accessible by your other computers that are logged in to the same account.

Important note: This feature is still experimental as there are some quirks about how the sync mechanism works. For best results, perform a manual sync before creating a backup, then create your backup, then perform a manual sync again.
